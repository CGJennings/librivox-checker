Track Length

A text is usually split into sections that are 10 to 40 minutes long, to break them up into convenient-sized chunks for listening and writing to CDs. There are some exceptions to this. For example, most poems are shorter than 10 minutes, so this guideline would not apply. If you need help deciding how to break up your file, or if you are not sure if this guideline applies to your section, contact the book coordinator (BC) for the project.
